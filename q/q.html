Quora Project

Make Quora more useful for “How To” Q & As. 

8,330,000,000 results!

The Five Ws, Five Ws and one H, or the Six Ws are questions whose answers are considered basic in information-gathering. They are often mentioned in journalism, research, and police investigations.[1] They constitute a formula for getting the complete story on a subject.[2] 

 

Some authors add a sixth question, “how”, to the list, though "how" can also be covered by "what", "when", or "where".

Goals in Questioning:

Enabling an Answerer to best grasp what I'm trying to learn and communicate their knowledge in the best way/medium possible.

 

Goals in Answering:

Enable the Questioner to easily grasp and best understand what I'm trying to convey, in a medium they can best learn from.

Principles for Questioning and Answering

From questions bloom answers – Some good, some not so good. How can answers be made better?

 

The question-asking experience must be improved before we can obtain a better answering/answer-viewing experience.
